COMPLIANCE REFERENCE
Key clauses referenced in this schema
HIPAA §164.312(b) — Audit Controls
Hardware, software, and procedural mechanisms to record and examine activity in systems containing ePHI. → AUDIT_LOGS, PHI_ACCESS_LOGS
HIPAA §164.308(a)(4) — Access Control
Unique user identification and access controls based on minimum necessary principle. → ROLES, USER_ROLES, REFRESH_TOKENS
HIPAA §164.528 — Accounting of Disclosures
Individuals have right to receive accounting of disclosures of their PHI. → PHI_ACCESS_LOGS
GDPR Art.17 — Right to Erasure
Data must be erasable on request without breaking system integrity. → anonymized_at on USERS, soft deletes everywhere
GDPR Art.6 & Art.7 — Lawful Basis & Consent
Processing must have a documented lawful basis; consent must be freely given, specific, and withdrawable. → USER_CONSENTS
GDPR Art.5(2) — Accountability
Controller must demonstrate compliance with data protection principles. → AUDIT_LOGS (write-only, immutable)
IRDAI (Registration, Certification and Operations of Insurers) Regulations, 2024
Unified framework governing intermediary licensing, certifications, registration lifecycle tracking, corporate compliance protocols, and commission governance models. → AGENTS, ROLES, BROKER_QUALIFIED_PERSONS, COMPLIANCE_MONEY_LEDGER
IRDAI (Protection of Policyholders’ Interests, Operations and Allied Matters) Regulations, 2024
Consolidated rules governing strict SLA-backed turnaround limits (30-day settlement targets), mandatory communication triggers, electronic product structures, and data disclosures. → CLAIMS, POLICIES, NOTIFICATIONS, DOCUMENTS, PREMIUM_SCHEDULE
